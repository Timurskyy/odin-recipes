Creamy Pulled Pork Soup

Warm up from the inside out with this cozy soup that comes together quickly and
easily with milk, spices, chicken broth, barbecue sauce, and leftover pulled pork.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 ¾ cups cooked pulled pork
  • 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley

    • Steps :

    1. Combine milk and chicken broth in a large pot. Add pulled pork and barbecue sauce
      and stir. Stir in chili powder, black pepper, oregano, and salt. Let simmer over low
      heat, stirring occasionally, for 45 minutes.

    2. Divide into 6 bowls and top with parsley.
